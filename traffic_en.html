Freediving Experience

“With one breath, meet your purest self.”

Freediving is not a contest of holding breath or depth—it is a quiet dialogue between body, breath, and sea.
No prior diving or strong swimming ability needed. Release tension; let the water support you and discover trust in stillness.
At RiShima Dive you begin at the surface and gradually descend—perhaps sharing a moment with a sea turtle beneath beams of sun.
